Welcome

Codez Pro is a Pakistani software development company. We offer multiple services for website development, custom software development, and mobile application development. Our additional services also include API development. We serve our clients with the maximum dedication and responsibility to preserve the quality feasible courting with them.

We are aware that creating client-oriented software takes a mixture of technical excellence and clear communication and we ensure you receive both. We know that every client is unique and we strive to deliver an individual, innovative and affordable proposal every time and to follow it through with an outstanding delivery which is both on time and within budget.

Why us?

Our approach

  • What we're doing?
  • Why Codez Pro?

We treat your project like it’s our own

When we onboard you as a new client, working with your project is a full-time job. Instead of just starting working on it, we study your industry, thoroughly review your requirements and your existing content, spend hours or even days in order to come up with the best possible solution. It’s a lot. And it’s the exact same thing we do for our own project. Our goal is to give your project the same care, quality, and love that we put into our own project, and to make you more money than you’re spending.

We know what we're doing

Between the experts on our team, we have good experience of running scalable applications, coding, scaling websites, designing graphics, and achieving results. With most of our clients, our software development efforts tend to blend into their efforts, and we’ll help them out with many things that are cost efficient solutions, best suitable third party libraries and best practices to secure their website even though it’s not necessarily part of their campaign. When you work with us, you’re working with a team of experts who practice advanced software development for fun. We live and breathe software development, and we love what we do!

We do all of the heavy lifting

Our goal is to handle as many aspects of the software development process as we can. Some of our clients work with us with a more hands-on approach, such as defining sprint deadlines, providing us third party solutions of their own choice, providing design prototypes etc. Others prefer to let us handle everything from start to finish in a way that is completely hands-off. We are very flexible and work well under either situation.

We're honest and we spoil our clients

Honesty is another one of our core values. We’ve built a reputation through honesty and communication with our clients. We’re not in the churn-and-burn business of constantly replacing unhappy dropped clients with new ones every few months. We work long hours so our clients see the results of our efforts and stick with us for years to come. If something is hurting your site, we’ll let you know about it. For example, we’ve discovered hidden spam links nestled in the source code of our client’s sites from their previous shady web developers, and we’ve discovered sites that were suffering from algorithmic penalties due to low-quality pages that were accidentally being indexed. These aren’t easy things to bring up to clients, but we believe honesty is the best policy, even if it means risking losing those business relationships. We tell it how it is

We're available on multiple channels

When you’re working with us, you’re not dealing with a team of developers or creating support tickets on some website, you’re working directly with the higher management. We believe communication preferences should be made by the customer, not by the merchant. For this reason, we’re accessible by any of your preferred methods; phone, email, text, instant messenger, and even in-person meets. We have customers who we only speak to through email, and others that like to text us with updates from their phones. Some only prefer phone calls. Others use all three. The important part is, we’re here for you 7 days a week. Add us to your speed dial. Many people undervalue the importance of communication and accessibility, but from what we’ve heard from our clients and how much they appreciate that they can text or call us, even on the weekends, we thought we’d list that here.
